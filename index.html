Individual Taxpayer Services

PIN Registration

The Personal Identification Number (PIN) is a mandatory requirement for all taxpayers in Kenya. Apply for your PIN online through our iTax portal.

  • New PIN registration for Kenyan citizens
  • Alien PIN registration for non-citizens
  • Company PIN registration
  • PIN retrieval and certificate reprinting

Tax Obligations

Understand your tax obligations as an individual taxpayer:

  • Income Tax: For employed and self-employed individuals
  • Turnover Tax (TOT): For businesses with turnover between KSh 1M and KSh 25M
  • Monthly Rental Income (MRI): For rental income earners
  • Capital Gains Tax: On transfer of property

Returns Filing

File your tax returns easily through iTax. Deadlines:

  • Individual Income Tax: 30th June annually
  • Turnover Tax: 20th of each month
  • Monthly Rental Income: 20th of each month
  • Nil returns can be filed for non-active taxpayers

Business Services

Business Registration

Register your business for tax purposes and obtain necessary tax obligations based on your business type.

  • Sole proprietorship registration
  • Partnership registration
  • Corporate entity registration
  • NGO and charitable organization registration

Value Added Tax (VAT)

VAT is charged on supply of taxable goods and services. Current VAT rate is 16%.

  • Mandatory registration for businesses with turnover above KSh 5M annually
  • Voluntary registration available below threshold
  • Monthly VAT returns due by 20th of following month
  • Electronic Tax Invoice Management System (eTIMS) compliance required

Excise Duty

Excise duty is imposed on specific goods manufactured in or imported into Kenya:

  • Alcoholic beverages and tobacco products
  • Petroleum products
  • Motor vehicles
  • Cosmetics and beauty products
  • Financial services (excise duty on fees and commissions)

About KRA

Who We Are

The Kenya Revenue Authority (KRA) was established by an Act of Parliament, the Kenya Revenue Authority Act, which became effective on 1 July 1995. We are responsible for the assessment, collection and accounting of all revenues due to the Government of Kenya.

Our vision is to be the leading revenue authority in the world, recognized for professionalism, integrity, and fairness.

Our Mandate

  • To assess, collect and account for all revenues in accordance with the written laws
  • To advise on matters relating to administration and collection of revenue
  • To perform such other functions relating to revenue as directed by the Cabinet Secretary
  • To combat tax evasion and revenue leakage
  • To facilitate trade and movement of people across borders
